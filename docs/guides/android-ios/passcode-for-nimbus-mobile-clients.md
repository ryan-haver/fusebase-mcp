# Passcode for FuseBase mobile clients

Often, pages are needed to store important personal data. For extra security, [Nimbus Note](https://thefusebase.com/note/) has an option to set a passcode. Once the passcode is set, then it will be impossible to open the application without it.

**☝** **You can only set a passcode for mobile versions of Nimbus Note.**

![Once the passcode is set, then it will be impossible to open the application without it.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4586051/xaa721u3s4b6gkiaxhub/S1vhTjjyGctNZ0Ft/1%20(9).gif)

## How to set a passcode [#](#b429660488_587)

You need to do the following:

-   Go to **Settings.**
-   Choose **Protect app with passcode**.
-   Set a 4-digit passcode.
-   Confirm the passcode.

![How to set a passcode](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4586051/xaa721u3s4b6gkiaxhub/4QEX5UVmMPT2fVkU/2%20(9).gif)

## Manage your passcode [#](#b1690836575_557)

### When to require passcode? [#](#b1690836575_605)

The application will ask for a passcode depending on which interval you have chosen.

-   immediately
-   after 5 minutes
-   after 15 minutes
-   after 30 minutes
-   after 1 hour
-   after 6 hours

**☝** **By default, the passcode is asked after any exit from the application, even if it’s just for 5 seconds.**

### Delete passcode [#](#b1690836575_1017)

To delete a passcode, do the following.

-   Click on **Delete passcode** or move the toggle to inactive position.
-   Enter the current passcode.

## 2-Step Verification [#](#b1288945058_29)

Two-Step verification is designed to increase the security of your data.

You can set **2-Step Verification** in the web client. Then after entering your login / password on the mobile client, you will need to enter the code.

![Two-Step verification is designed to increase the security of your data.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4586051/xaa721u3s4b6gkiaxhub/NP0nRZMGLTmQh6v4/Group%201%20(39).png)
