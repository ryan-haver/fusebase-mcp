# Adding VideoAsk widget to public pages

Not so long ago, the TypeForm team launched a new service [VideoAsk.com](https://www.videoask.com/), which allows you to place special widgets on websites to collect visual feedback.  
Today we will show you how to add VideoAsk widget to [FuseBase](https://thefusebase.com/) public pages.

> This method will only work for users of the business plan.

-   Log in to your VideoAsk account or create a new account.
-   Open your VideoAsk or create a new one.
-   Click on **Share & Embed**.

![Today we will show you how to add VideoAsk widget to Nimbus Note public pages.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/o37WHSQlI4AgcxMc/Group%20193%20(1).png)

-   Select **Widget** and customize it.

![Select Widget and customize it.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/gxQQggrfn6UgZFLI/Group%20194%20(1).png)

-   Press on **Get the code** and **copy the code**.

![Press on Get the code and copy the code.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/kTktcl0HRvz9qLpx/Group%20195%20(1).png)

-   Open the business console of your organization in FuseBase – [https://teams.nimbusweb.me/](https://teams.nimbusweb.me/)
-   Open the workspace management page and select **Custom HTML / JS** from the menu of the desired workspace.

![Open the workspace management page and select Custom HTML / JS from the menu of the desired workspace.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/AznRagCGVWqbZ73o/Group%20196%20(1).png)

-   Click on **Add HTML/JS**.

![Click on Add HTML/JS.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/tPMrlOr3MpMpGqfs/Group%20197%20(1).png)

-   Enter the code from step 5 in the Body field and click on **Publish**.

![Enter the code from step 5 in the Body field and click on Publish .](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/C60NRI6YIZMG5Po8/Group%20198%20(2).png)

-   Open a public link from this workspace and check for the display of the widget.

![Open a public link from this workspace and check for the display of the widget.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5479579/3peabp382g8nzbhhnkts/xmwmNcGNsfiXZ1xO/Group%20199%20(1).png)

Everything should be good and working correctly!
