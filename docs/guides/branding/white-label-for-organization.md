# White label for organization

In [Fusebase](https://thefusebase.com/) you can set a logo for your organization. This will later be displayed in the client and in embeds. You can also set a logo for the login page.

## Organization branding [#](#b172420923_150)

### Organization logo [#](#b3338586401_26)

To set an organization logo you need to:

-   Open the business organization dashboard at [https://teams.nimbusweb.me](https://teams.nimbusweb.me/).
-   Select **Settings**.

![Organization logo](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5030466/z6snpc459i71ho4u18g2/mLuMfFRkG45yLhfE/image.png)

-   Click on **Organization branding**.

![Organization logo](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5030466/z6snpc459i71ho4u18g2/UetDnNl3dnliEnzG/Group%20192.png)

-   Upload **small logo** to display the logo in the organization settings, embedded objects, etc.

![Upload small logo to display the logo in the organization settings, embedded objects, etc.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5030466/z6snpc459i71ho4u18g2/ODDvhL1x0Qck6AEt/Group%20193.png)

-   Upload **big logo** to display on the sign-in page, error pages, etc.

![](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-21-1024x786.png)

**☝ Note: If you have added a big logo of the organization and indicated its name, then this data will be used in emails sent to members of the organization – for instance, when you invite members or mention them in pages.**

![ If you have added a big logo of the organization and indicated its name, then this data will be used in emails sent to members of the organization - for instance, when you invite members or mention them in pages.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5030466/z6snpc459i71ho4u18g2/CoASeTs5mCbKMkQp/540_2041_branding_logo_new.gif)

### Authorization page branding [#](#b3338586401_277)

![Authorization page branding](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5030466/z6snpc459i71ho4u18g2/WGUxNs4YQvLBJLuj/1106_2022_br.gif)

-   Write a welcome text for members of the organization (instead of the default standard welcome).
-   Provide your link for the Privacy Policy (or remove it).

![Authorization page branding](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5030466/z6snpc459i71ho4u18g2/XvP3LSBBGI62zKDM/Group%20195.png)
