# Forbidding of editing of individual blocks

You can forbid editing of individual blocks on a page, which is especially important if you have a lot of members in your workspaces and you do not want important content to be accidentally deleted.

> Lock blocks are available only to users of the business plan.

![In Nimbus Note, you can forbid editing of individual blocks in a page, which is especially important if you have a lot of members in your workspaces and you do not want important content to be accidentally deleted.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5224247/cubds7xg5k0bul3cdtd0/WXSyV2O85MISJF8M/540_2lockk_2010.gif)

-   Click on the **block menu**.
-   Select **Lock block** to deny editing.

![Select Lock block to deny editing.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5224247/cubds7xg5k0bul3cdtd0/wtWXsE9FTsfUtTvL/Group%20199.png)

-   Click on the lock icon to unlock the block.

![Click on the lock icon to unlock the block.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5224247/cubds7xg5k0bul3cdtd0/fEfiQklsQfsyGKMI/Group%20200.png)

**☝ Note: You can also disable editing for multiple blocks at once. Just select them and change the setting in the menu.**

![You can also disable editing for multiple blocks at once. Just select them and change the setting in the menu.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5224247/cubds7xg5k0bul3cdtd0/GVb4A4BOLn8kzc4u/Group%20201.png)

> Only owners / managers of the organization and workspace admins have access to locking block edits.
