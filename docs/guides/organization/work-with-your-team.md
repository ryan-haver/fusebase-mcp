**Build dream collaboration for your dream team. On this page, you’ll find the most efficient tips for setting up your internal work. Let’s get started!**

##  Collaborate Everywhere: Text, Tables, Pictures, Embeds [#](#0-toc-title)

You can suggest your ideas and share feedback using comments in FuseBase.  
Add them anywhere on the page — content blocks, inline text, images and tasks. Our videos below will show you how comments work.

🔗 You can always find more FuseBase tips on [our YouTube channel >>](https://www.youtube.com/@NimbusWebInc)

## How to Add Comments to Your Pages [#](#1-toc-title)

Leave [comments](https://thefusebase.com/guides/basics/comments-inside-the-pages/) anywhere in FuseBase— on pages, images, tables, and more. Discuss ideas, share feedback, and resolve threads.

To add comments in FuseBase:

1.  Click directly on the image where you want to comment.
2.  The comment window will open pinned to that spot.
3.  Type your feedback in the comment field.
4.  Tag teammates to loop them in @name.
5.  Mark resolved when the discussion is finished.

😎 **FuseBase Team suggests**  
Try a couple of the small hacks below to make your **commenting** process even easier.

-   Mark comments as ***Resolved*** when the discussion is over.
-   Navigate between page comments with arrows.
-   Keep your discussions within the page and your ideas and feedback won’t be missed.
-   Tag subject matter experts to loop them in.
-   Enable notifications for important mentions and events

##  Communicate Where the Work Happens — Chats in FuseBase [#](#5-toc-title)

Find the [Chat option](https://thefusebase.com/guides/basics/chat-in-nimbus-note/) in your left sidebar. With it, you can concentrate discussions within particular workspaces, sending direct messages to your teammates and creating public or private channels for quick discussions.

😎 **FuseBase Team** suggests  
Create channels for each of your initiatives. While keeping your ideas and feedback in the **comment** on pages, get quick responses via **chats**.

##  Connect People, Pages, and Folders using Mentions [#](#7-toc-title)

Navigate within your content and keep everyone updated using mentions. Just type **@** to mention people or reference workspaces, folders, and pages. You can easily switch between discussions, read more info on desired topics or notify your teammates about changes.

😎 **FuseBase Team suggests**  
Add dates via **@-mention** to specify deadlines inside your text and add alerts to remind you and your teammates about them.
