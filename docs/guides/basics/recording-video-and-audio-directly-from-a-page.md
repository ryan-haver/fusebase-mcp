# Recording video and audio directly from a page

## Recording audio [#](#b4218177677_1072)

-   Open [FuseBase](https://thefusebase.com/) and create new page.
-   Click on the arrow and select **Audio** **Record** or **cmd/ctrl** \+ **⌥/alt** + **8**.

![Recording audio](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/iLAsFpT9KCIhoQWk/Group%20260.png)

-   **Give permission** to use the microphone.

![Give permission to use the microphone.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/E1Q9587xfmavF8K2/Group%20216%20(1).png)

-   Audio Recording will start.

![](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/000-1024x657.png)

### How do I stop / pause / delete while recording? [#](#b4218177677_1018)

-   You can pause by clicking **Pause recording** and then continue by clicking **Resume recording**.

![You can pause by clicking Pause recording and then continue by clicking Resume recording.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/RUIkcaEdgSVq8s1n/Group%20246.png)

-   To cancel recording, click **Cancel recording**.

![To cancel recording, click Cancel recording](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/mquIUodLQie1qZrc/Group%20247.png)

-   To stop recording, click **Stop recording**.

![To stop recording, click Stop recording.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/i7TMu6RQF2uBfpAy/Group%20245.png)

☝ If the audio recording is interrupted for some reason, you can restore or delete it.

![ If the audio recording is interrupted for some reason, you can restore or delete it.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/ZpNOVnSxAn9NTfYc/Group%20259.png)

### Lifehack [#](#b4218177677_778)

During audio playback, you can click on different parts of the recording to start playing from the selected time.

You can also change the volume: increase, decrease or completely mute the sound.

![During audio playback, you can click on different parts of the recording to start playing from the selected time.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/P9txqCaNQNL7IBuc/Group%20248.png)

☝ For free workspaces, the duration limit for recorded files (audio/video) is 5 minutes.

## Recording video [#](#b4218177677_1093)

Record audio and videos right in your page! Just a couple of clicks and your audio ? or video ? message is in your page! In a video, you can record both your desktop and your webcam image.

Use these features to add information on the go and give more context.

-   Click on the arrow and select **Video Record** or **cmd/ctrl** + **⌥/alt** + **9**.

![In a video, you can record both your desktop and your webcam image.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/uVI9f8211UWe36km/Group%20249.png)

-   Select **Screen** + **Cam**/**Screen only**/**Cam only**.
-   **Screen + Cam** – the screen and webcam will be recorded (will be active only on the client’s page).
-   **Screen** – video from the screen will be recorded.
-   **Cam** – only the video from the webcam will be recorded.

![Use these features to add information on the go and give more context.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/T2W2ky9VWumWUAi4/Group%20250.png)

-   **Give permission** to use microphone / camera.

![Give permission to use microphone / camera.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/7090HELYmkFSWMic/Group%20217%20(1).png)

### Permissions for Mac users [#](#b1995924032_670)

-   Click on the **Apple logo** on the top left of the menu bar.
-   Click on **System Preferences**
-   Click on the **Security & Privacy icon**.

![Permissions for Mac users](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/WC9oDX99Q2psVJYD/Group%20226.png)

-   From the modal, scroll down to **Screen Recording section**.

![Permissions for Mac users](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5607239/l55bgak2ivs9bfptruka/4LIpBYqBpIF7x0Ob/Group%20227.png)

-   Click **Quit now** and **restart the Browser.**

## How do I regain access to my microphone or camera? [#](#8-toc-title)

If for some reason you blocked FuseBase’s access to your microphone or camera, then you can quickly restore it.

### Regaining access to a microphone [#](#b2964993421_118)

-   Click on the icon for lock permissions.

![Regaining access to a microphone](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5441210/4qccr41slk6wsw61h5fb/kMofVQNAtXg8k6V3/Group%20215.png)

-   Next, switch to Always allow and click Done.

![Regaining access to a microphone](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5441210/4qccr41slk6wsw61h5fb/K4GwCkgeuHmH4QAq/Group%20216.png)

### Regaining access to a camera [#](#b2964993421_324)

-   Click on the icon for lock permissions.

![Regaining access to a camera](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5441210/4qccr41slk6wsw61h5fb/52Y2fu9dAJpBdprh/Group%20215.png)

-   Next, switch to Always allow and click Done.

![Regaining access to a camera](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/5441210/4qccr41slk6wsw61h5fb/qRRon9guqp8sc2bL/Group%20217.png)
