# How do I change the account email?

1) Open the web client.

2) Go to Settings –

![](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Frame-2147207153-534x1024.png)

3) Next to the account email, click Change –

![How do I change the account email?](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Frame-2147207152-3-1024x734.png)

4) Specify the new email and confirm it.
