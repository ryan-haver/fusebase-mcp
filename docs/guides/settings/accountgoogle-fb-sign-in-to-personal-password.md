# Account|Google/FB sign-in to personal password

You can always set a password for your account, even if you registered through **Sign in with Google / Facebook**

-   open the main page of [Fusebase](https://thefusebase.com/) and click on **Restore Password**

![](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-8-1024x906.png)

-   then enter your email address and we will send you instructions on how to restore your password

![](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-9-1024x736.png)

-   check your inbox, follow the link and set a password

![](https://thefusebase.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-10-1024x514.png)
