# Why don’t I hear sound after converting into MP4?

In all likelihood, it has to do with an audio codec issue.  
1) Download and install codec packs:  
For Windows – [https://codecguide.com/download\_k-lite\_codec\_pack\_mega.htm](https://codecguide.com/download_k-lite_codec_pack_mega.htm)

![In all likelihood, it has to do with an audio codec issue.  Download and install codec packs](https://thefusebase.com/images/klite.jpg)

For MAC – [https://perian.org](https://perian.org)

![ Download and install codec packs](https://thefusebase.com/images/perianmac.jpg)

2) After installation, reboot the system.  
3) Try playing the video again.
