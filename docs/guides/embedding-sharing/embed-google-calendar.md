# Embed Google Calendar

You can add an embed of your Google Calendar to your page in FuseBase page

First get a link to the desired calendar.

-   Open your [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/).
-   Select calendar and click on three dots menu.

![Select calendar and click on three dots menu.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4630869/odk1c50h54o4eio6r5yb/tYDczpl7p3dJT1MU/Group%2018%20(4).png)

-   Click on **Settings and sharing**.

![Click on Settings and sharing.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4630869/odk1c50h54o4eio6r5yb/reRZAKKi1BLOLXhm/Group%2019%20(4).png)

-   Go to **Access Permissions**.
-   Select **Make available to public**.

![Select Make available to public.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4630869/odk1c50h54o4eio6r5yb/3weag8o4K2xP50GB/Group%2020%20(4).png)

-   Go to **Integrate calendar** and copy the url or embed code.

![Go to Integrate calendar and copy the url or embed code.](https://app.nimbusweb.me/box/attachment/4630869/odk1c50h54o4eio6r5yb/m9eTVLrcdJFwNFOw/Group%2021%20(5).png)

In [FuseBase](https://thefusebase.com/) you can insert an embed in several ways:

-   Copy the URL from Google Calendar.
    -   Paste it into the page as an Embed using right click **Paste** or **Ctrl+V**.

-   Copy the Embed code.
    -   Click on the arrow and select **Embed** or **cmd/ctrl** + **⌥/alt** **+** **m**.
    -   Paste the code through Ctrl+V.
